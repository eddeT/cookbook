# Rehab

## Number 1
### Summary
Do this routine at least two times.

### Routine:
 * 5 walkouts to push up
 * 12 Alternating Lunges
 * 10 Squats
 * 12 Alternating deadlifts
 * 12 Hip Lifts
 * 12 Alternating plank walk, slowly
 * 25 Heel Raises
 * 25 Toe Raises
 * 10 L Heel Lifts
 * 10 R Heel Lifts
 * 10 Slow breaths prayer position
