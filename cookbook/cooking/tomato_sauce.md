# Tomato Sauce

## Pizza saucue
### Summary
Good sauce for pizza or a simple pasta.

### Recipe
* Olive oil
* 4 Cloves garlic
* 3 Schallots
* Dried Oregano
* Dried Basil
* Black Pepper
* 1 tsp salt

Fry in pan until fragrant

Add 2 cans crushed tomatoes

Finish up with some fresh herbs, more olive oil and salt to taste.
